Peer Teacher

Peer teaching to me is a way to give quality help to students in need. I became a peer teacher because I also once utilized this resource as a way for me to get help in some of my assignments and to reinforce some of the knowledge from my classes. I cannot stress how helpful the peer teachers were when I needed them. Thus I would like to provide that same help to the future generation of aggies, as a way to help with their learning and understanding of the Computer Science field.
